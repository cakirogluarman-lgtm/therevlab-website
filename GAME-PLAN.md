# Revlab — Launch Game Plan

PPF · Color Wraps · Detailing — Boca Raton, FL

The goal of the first 60 days: look like the most premium shop in the area before you've spent a dollar on ads, then turn that polish into booked appointments. Everything below is sequenced so each step feeds the next.

---

## Phase 0 — Foundation (this week)

Lock the basics so every piece of content and every click has somewhere to land.

- **Name & handle:** Use one consistent handle everywhere — `@therevlab` (IG, TikTok, YouTube, Google). Grab them all today even if you only post on one at first.
- **Website live:** The site in this folder goes up at `therevlab.com`. It's the booking engine and the proof — point every bio link and every DM to it.
- **Google Business Profile:** Create it for the Boca Raton location. This is the single highest-ROI free thing you can do for a local shop — it puts you on Maps and in "PPF near me" searches. Add hours, services, and photos.
- **Booking + payments:** Stripe is wired into the site (deposit on booking). Connect your Stripe account and you can take real bookings on day one.

## Phase 1 — Content engine (week 1–2)

You can't promote an empty page. Shoot first, launch second. You're a content company that happens to wrap cars — that mindset wins on social.

**The first shoot (one full day at the shop):**

- A full PPF install, start to finish — squeegee passes, the wet film catching light, the final reveal wipe-down.
- A color wrap reveal — before/after, the door panel peel, a slow roll-out of the finished car.
- Detailing money shots — foam cannon, paint correction swirl-then-gloss, ceramic water beading.
- Macro/texture B-roll — film edge tucking, polisher pad spinning, water sheeting off a fresh coat. This is the stuff that goes viral.
- One "founder" clip — you, 15 seconds, why you opened Revlab. People book people.

**Gear:** A phone in 4K 60fps is enough to start. Shoot on a gimbal if you have one, keep the shop clean and dark with one strong key light — the premium look is *contrast*, not brightness.

**Editing rhythm:** Cut every shoot into 8–12 vertical reels. One satisfying-process reel can carry the whole launch.

## Phase 2 — Social launch (week 2–3)

- **Post cadence:** 4–5 reels/week to start. Consistency beats perfection.
- **Format that works for this niche:** satisfying process clips (the squeegee/peel) + dramatic reveals + a hook in the first 1.5 seconds ("Watch this $90k car disappear under film").
- **Captions:** short, confident, end with a soft CTA — "Booking June installs now — link in bio."
- **Hashtags/geo:** tag Boca Raton, Delray, Boca, Palm Beach. Local discovery is everything for a garage.
- **First "proof" posts:** every car you do, post it. Tag the owner and the car's brand community — that's free reach into exactly the right audience.

## Phase 3 — Get the first clients (week 3–6)

- **Warm network first:** Post in local car groups and Boca/Palm Beach Facebook groups. Offer a founding-client rate for the first 5 cars in exchange for filming rights.
- **Partnerships:** Boca has exotic/luxury dealers, indie used-luxury lots, and car clubs. Offer them wholesale PPF on inventory + a referral cut. One dealer relationship can fill your calendar.
- **The reveal loop:** Every finished car becomes content → content drives DMs → DMs go to the booking page → booked deposit. That's the whole machine.
- **Reviews:** Ask every one of the first clients for a Google review the day you hand back the keys. Five 5-star reviews makes you look established.
- **Referrals:** "$100 off your next service for every friend you send." Car people travel in packs.

## Phase 4 — Scale (week 6+)

- Turn the best-performing reels into paid ads (Instagram/TikTok) geo-fenced to a 25-mile radius of Boca. You'll already know which content converts because the organic posts told you.
- Add a simple email/text follow-up so quote requests don't go cold.
- Consider a Coastmotive-style monthly content retainer for yourself — you're already shooting; systematize it.

---

## 30-day checklist

1. Reserve `@therevlab` on every platform + buy/point `therevlab.com`.
2. Launch this website + connect Stripe.
3. Set up Google Business Profile.
4. Shoot day one (PPF + wrap + detail + B-roll + founder clip).
5. Cut 10 reels, schedule the first week.
6. Post daily, reply to every comment and DM within an hour.
7. Lock 5 founding clients at a launch rate, film all of them.
8. Collect 5 Google reviews.
9. Approach 2 local dealers / car clubs for a partnership.
10. Boost the single best reel as a geo-targeted ad.

The website does the heavy lifting on credibility and booking — your job is to keep feeding the content engine. Reveal, post, book, repeat.
